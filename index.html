I Am Happy Project Batna

What we do?

We are a group of Young Batnean people that agreed to say ENOUGH to negativity, and start spreading Happiness in the area. Together in collaboration with Mr Edwin Edebiri the Chief Happiness Officer of I am Happy Project, we are planning to Help people find out their own Happiness and share it in Order to create a Happier Society.

How we do it?

By organizing monthly meetings where Happy people can share ideas that –according to them- can spread "happiness" in the area.
Actions that demonstrate the selected ideas during the meetings.
Events: Based on the question « what makes you happy ? », asking people from different categories to come and share their own Happiness (a Memory, a Song, a Picture...etc)

Why we do what we do?

Joy is contagious. If people see you making the initiative towards a happier life, you can motivate others to do the same. You can help others gain positivity, which –in the other hand- leads to increasiging productivity, and a greater sense of well-being.
